Save Our Oceans: Stop Deep-Sea Mining

The Problem

Deep-sea mining threatens fragile ecosystems, harming marine biodiversity and disrupting the ocean's natural balance. Machines stir up sediment, destroy habitats, and emit harmful radiation that affects marine life.

Our Solution: Project Himaya

Project Himaya uses an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) to selectively harvest polymetallic nodules without disturbing marine ecosystems. Key features include:

  • Precision pick-and-place claw
  • AI-powered imaging to detect marine life
  • Environmentally friendly design with safety features
